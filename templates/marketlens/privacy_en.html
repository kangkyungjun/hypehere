MarketLens Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

MarketLens (hereinafter "Company") values the personal information of its users and complies with applicable laws and regulations, including the Personal Information Protection Act and the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection.

Article 1 (Personal Information Collected)

The Company collects the following personal information to provide its services:

1.1 Required Information (Email-based Registration)

  • Email address: Used for account identification and login
  • Password: Stored in encrypted form
  • Nickname: Display name within the service

1.2 Information Collected via Social Login (Planned)

The Company plans to offer social login via Google, Apple, and other providers (OAuth 2.0) in the future. The following information may be collected when using social login:

  • Google Login: Email address, profile photo URL, name (within the scope provided by Google OAuth API)
  • Apple Login: Email address, user identifier (within the scope provided by Apple Sign In)
  • Purpose of collection: Account creation, identity verification, prevention of duplicate registrations
  • Retention period: Until account deletion

Information collected through social login is processed in accordance with each platform's privacy policy, and the Company collects only the minimum necessary information.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information: Mobile device identifier (advertising ID), OS version
  • Service usage records: Access logs, usage duration, visit history
  • Advertising identifier: Personalized advertising through Google AdMob

1.4 Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Company does not currently use WebView; however, WebView or browser-based features may be added in the future to improve services. In such cases, the following information may be collected:

  • Cookies: Session maintenance, login state storage
  • Local storage: User preference storage
  • Purpose of collection: Service optimization, user experience improvement
  • How to opt out: Cookies can be blocked through browser settings (note: some features may be limited)

Article 2 (Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information)

The Company uses the collected personal information for the following purposes:

  • Member management: Member registration and identity verification, member identification and account management, prevention of fraudulent use
  • Service provision: AI-based stock analysis data provision, personalized content recommendations, customer inquiry support
  • Marketing and advertising: Personalized advertising through Google AdMob, statistical analysis for service improvement
  • Legal compliance: Fulfillment of obligations under applicable laws, dispute resolution and complaint handling

Article 3 (Retention Period of Personal Information)

In principle, the Company destroys personal information without delay once the purpose of collection and use has been fulfilled. However, where retention is required under applicable laws, the information is retained for the following periods:

  • Upon account deletion: Immediate destruction (except where retention is required by law)
  • Records of fraudulent use: 5 years (Electronic Commerce Act)
  • Service usage records: 3 months (Protection of Communications Secrets Act)

Article 4 (Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties)

In principle, the Company does not provide users' personal information to third parties. However, exceptions are made in the following cases:

4.1 Google AdMob (Advertising Service)

  • Recipient: Google LLC
  • Information provided: Advertising identifier (GAID/IDFA), device information
  • Purpose of provision: Personalized advertising
  • Retention period: In accordance with Google's Privacy Policy
  • How to opt out: Enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in device settings

4.2 Legal Requirements

  • When requested by investigative authorities pursuant to applicable laws
  • When the user has given explicit consent

Article 5 (Destruction Procedures and Methods for Personal Information)

The Company destroys personal information as follows when the retention period has expired or the purpose of processing has been achieved:

  • Electronic files: Permanently deleted using methods that prevent recovery
  • Paper documents: Shredded or incinerated

Article 6 (User Rights)

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request to view personal information: Can be viewed in the 'Settings' menu within the app
  • Correction of personal information: Can be corrected directly within the app
  • Deletion of personal information and account withdrawal: Use the 'Delete Account' feature within the app
  • Request to suspend processing of personal information: Can be requested through customer support

The Company will notify the user of the processing results within 10 days of receiving the request.

Article 7 (Security Measures for Personal Information)

The Company takes the following measures to protect personal information:

  • Encryption: Passwords are encrypted using secure methods such as bcrypt
  • Access control: Minimized access privileges to personal information processing systems
  • Security programs: Regular security audits and updates

Article 8 (Privacy Officer)

For inquiries regarding personal information, please contact us at:

  • Email: marketlens.service@gmail.com
  • Company: HypeHere (Operating MarketLens Service)
  • Response time: Within 1-3 business days

Article 9 (Changes to Privacy Policy)

This Privacy Policy may be amended in accordance with applicable laws and Company policies. Any changes will be announced in advance through in-app notices or email notifications.
